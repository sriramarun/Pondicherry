An Invitation to Pause

AI can answer almost anything. The one conversation it can't have for you is the one with yourself.

Information is now instant and free. What stays rare is the time to sit with your own thinking — and decide where you're actually headed.

More meetings. More notifications. More pressure to keep up.

For one weekend, step away from the noise — to think, and to use AI not just to move faster, but to understand yourself better. This weekend makes the time.

Much of the value is the cohort itself — a small group of experienced professionals, each bringing a different perspective.

Step away — and think.

How is work changing?
What role should AI play in my professional life?
What am I learning? What am I building?
What do I want more of in the years ahead?

Saturday Morning
The Professional Audit & AI Leverage
Morning session · technology, AI & the future of work
Practical & Accessible
+

A practical, accessible session — no jargon, no prerequisites.

We'll explore
  • Audit where your time and energy actually go
  • Decide what to automate, augment, or drop
  • Define what remains your uniquely human "moat"
  • Use AI as a thinking partner — a mirror for clearer thinking, not just a shortcut
The goal is not to become an AI expert — it is to become a more thoughtful professional in an AI-enabled world.

Saturday Evening
Designing Your Next Chapter
Evening session
Guided Reflection
+

A gentle, guided reflection — not an exercise in reinvention, just an honest look at what matters.

We'll reflect on
  • What work gives you energy vs. what you have outgrown
  • Which skills to stop doing, and which human capabilities to strengthen
  • What opportunities excite you in the next 3–5 years
  • What you want more — or less — of in the years ahead
You'll capture your thinking in a personal Next Chapter Canvas to take home.
Sunday Morning
The 90-Day Blueprint & Closing Circle
Morning · before departure
Carry It Forward
+

Translate the weekend's insights into a practical roadmap. Each participant defines one major career decision, maps out a 30-day commitment, and is paired with an accountability partner from the cohort. We close with a final conversation before departure.
