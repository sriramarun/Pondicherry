Some questions deserve more than the gaps between meetings.** More meetings. More notifications. More content. More pressure to keep up. Yet the questions that shape the next few years of your working life rarely get the attention they deserve. This weekend makes the time. For one weekend, step away — and think. - How is work changing? - What role should AI play in my professional life? - What am I learning? What am I building? - What do I want more of in the years ahead? - Am I heading in the direction I truly want? - What deserves my attention now? --- ## Who It's For **Experienced professionals who are simply curious about what comes next.** For people with 10+ years of experience who are drawn to: - The future of work - Artificial Intelligence - Career growth & evolution - Meaningful professional connections - Personal reflection & renewal *No technical background required. No prior AI experience necessary. Only curiosity.* --- ## The Weekend *A gentle rhythm of conversation, exploration, and quiet. No presentations. No networking pressure. No workshops.* ### Friday Evening — Welcome Circle *Evening · over dinner* A relaxed conversation to get to know the group, share what brought us here, and set intentions for the weekend. > No presentations. No networking pressure. No workshops. Just conversation. ### Saturday Morning — AI & the Future of Work *Morning session · practical and accessible* A practical, accessible session — no jargon, no prerequisites.
